Who We Are

Yardsale! is a five piece indie rock outfit based out of Austin, Texas. Over the past year, they have made a name for themselves in the UT music scene through their raucous live performances and energetic studio material. Consisting of Alex Meeler on drums, Zach Russell on bass, Patrick Wang on keys, Christian Daniel on lead guitar, and Nathaniel Ogren on and lead vocals, Yardsale's energy and musicianship are not to be missed.
